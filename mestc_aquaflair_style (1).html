FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What does MESTC specialize in?
MESTC specializes in the wholesale supply of industrial and electrical products including explosion-proof equipment, hand tools, safety PPE, wires & cables, and oilfield supplies for UAE and GCC markets.
Are your products ATEX and IECEx certified?
Yes. All hazardous area products we supply are fully certified under ATEX and IECEx international standards, suitable for use in oil & gas, petrochemical and marine environments.
Do you offer bulk pricing and custom RFQ?
Absolutely. We offer competitive bulk pricing and custom RFQ for large orders. Submit your requirements and our team will respond within 24 hours with a detailed quotation.
Which industries do you serve?
We serve Oil & Gas, Construction, Marine & Offshore, Manufacturing, Energy & Power, and general industrial sectors across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait and Oman.
How fast can you deliver within the UAE?
We maintain local stock in Dubai and offer same-day and next-day delivery across the UAE. GCC deliveries are handled within 3–5 business days depending on location.
